Life before HTML & Java: My path to IT

Mid-high school was wild...deciding careers was exhilarating yet confusing, emotional for sure, and honestly stressful. All that while juggling matric premils and finals with some personal life chaos as well, can you imagine? Would you believe me if I told you that IT was my third choice after Law and Psychology? Well, my third choice turned into my first and I remember my first ever line of code and executing it was oddly heart warming. Little did I know that there would be nasty bugs along the way (if you know,you know). Anyway, that feeling of creating something out of nothing, those two words that formed a sentence that looked so basic, that's where it all started. Projects, new languages and unexpected errors that led to bugs and hours of debugging, where you later realised just as you head to bed that you ended a line with a colon instead of a semi-colon...those moments became my daily companions.

Let us dive into the chaos and small victories. Some things that would seem foreign to the outside world.
