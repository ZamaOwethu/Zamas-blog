Life before HTML & Java: My path to IT

Mid-high school was wild... deciding careers was exhilarating yet confusing, emotional for sure, and honestly stressful. All that while juggling matric prelims and finals with some personal life chaos as well. Would you believe me if I told you that IT was my third choice after Law and Psychology? Well, my third choice turned into my first, and I remember my first ever line of code and how executing it was oddly heartwarming. Little did I know there would be nasty bugs along the way (if you know, you know). Anyway, that feeling of creating something out of nothing, two words forming a sentence that looked so basics, that’s where it started. Projects, new languages, unexpected errors, hours of debugging, and then realizing as you lay on your bed that you ended a line with a colon instead of a semicolon… those moments became my daily companions.

LEt us dive into the chaos and small victories. Some things that would seem a little foreign to the outside world.
